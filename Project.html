About the Project

The (In)Visible Million Project is a collective effort to shed light on those overlooked by society.
Our mission is to map, visualize, and empower individuals who have remained invisible in mainstream narratives.
Through art, data, and participation, we aim to foster inclusion, transparency, and action.

What We Do

- Collect and share stories of the invisible.
- Create visual representations that make absence visible.
- Build community and invite participation.
- Advocate for systemic change.

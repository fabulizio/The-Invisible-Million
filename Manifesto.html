Our Manifesto

We envision a world where every individual—no matter how visible or invisible—counts.
The (In)Visible Million Project is dedicated to shining a light on those overlooked by society, fostering inclusion, transparency, and collective action.

Principles

Transparency: We share our process and results openly.
Community: Every voice matters; together we are stronger.
Impact: Small actions can create big change.
